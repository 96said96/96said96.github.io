CONCEPTION OF A SMART HOME MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
BASED ON IOT AND ML

End of Studies Internship

Smart home management system

This project presents an IoT and machine learning based smart home management system for home control, monitoring, comfort, safety, and energy saving. We used different home automation system in order to test different protocols in the field and their coexistence. We used the Loxone system, Sonoff & Aqara Zigbee devices and a WiFi based multi sensor in order to control and monitor the smart home, and Home Assistant app for the mobile and web interfaces and data collection. The LSTM machine learning algorithm used to learn temperature patterns in order control the HVAC system aiming to optimize energy consumption.

The brief

A project that integrates internet of objects and machine learning in the concept of smart homes building one eco-system that allows the control and monitoring of the house in additio to energy optimization.

IoT solution

This first part of the project consists in integrating the internet of things in the smart house, sensors and intelligent wireless actuators equipped with wifi and zigbee communication tools, gateway for the communication of the devices, the management of the commands and the actuators and also the management of the data on a cloud based database in addition to a personalised graphic interface to serve the control and the monitoring of the house

ML algorithm

The second part consists in integrating artificial intelligence tools in the energy management of the house with the use of prediction algorithms based on the historical data of the house (temperature, humidity, energy consumption ...) and the preferences of the user for the management of the hvac
